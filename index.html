Classic City Skatepark Alliance

At Classic City Skatepark Alliance, we know that skateparks enrich the lives of people in our community through recreation, exercise, engagement, and belonging. We are dedicated to increasing access to skatepark facilities in and around Athens Clarke County. We collaborate with public and private agencies to expand and create safe, high-quality skateparks.
